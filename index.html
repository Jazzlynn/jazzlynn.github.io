Marcel the Shell

Marcel the Shell is a youtube channel about, well, a shell named Marcel! I love Marcel because he has a great sense of humor, he is so smart, and super cute of course. Marcel talks about a number of different topics. He talks about his losing his sister, enjoying getting a feel for "daily life," and even sings a song. There are three episodes of Marcel the Shell. My friends and I love Marcel so much because he always makes us laugh.

In the episode of the series, Marcel of course inroduces himself. He immediately begins fumbling over his words mistaking his name as "Marshell." He also names some traits that he has such as shoes and being able to talk. So from the beginning Marcel is just this funny little shell. And the audience can relate to him because of his humor and the fact that he makes mistakes.

I think that everyone should watch Marcel the Shell because humor is the best type of medicine. This is also a cute mini series to watch with the entire family:) So far there are only three episodes, but I wish more episodes would be made.
